# CONTEXT.md — meta-seams/

**Meta-seams** are cross-silo standards on *how* a deliverable is made — shared Layer-3 reference,
applied by every silo. They shape output; they do not select material (that is each silo's own draw).

**Auto-applied, not opt-in.** A cold agent applies one whenever its trigger is present, in every silo,
without being told. They're distilled *rules*, not reference dumps.

Current meta-seams:
- **`writing.md`** — clarity + persuasion rules, paraphrased from *On Writing Well* (Zinsser) and the
  *Cult of Copy* almanac (Theriot). **Trigger: any deliverable that involves writing or persuasion →
  apply it automatically.** The writing bar every prose output must clear. Silos point to it from
  `reference/CONTEXT.md`.
