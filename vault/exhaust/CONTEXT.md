# CONTEXT.md: vault/exhaust/ (the well's record of closed arms)

When an arm closes, its `decision-log.md` is COPIED here (the original stays in the arm). One file
per closed arm: `exhaust/<arm-name>-decision-log.md`. Append-only; entries are never edited after
they land.

Exhaust entries are **records, not drawable material**: they get no rows in `../account.md` (the
catalogue stays for material silos and arms can draw). The one-way discipline holds — the well
receives the copy at close, it never reaches into an arm to take it.

Starts EMPTY (no arm has closed yet) and is correct empty.
